About

Swami Vivekananda was a great Indian monk and philosopher who introduced Indian spirituality and Yoga to the Western world. He inspired millions of people with his powerful thoughts and teachings.

Early Life

He was born on 12 January 1863 in Kolkata. His original name was Narendra Nath Datta. He was a bright student and later became a disciple of Sri Ramakrishna.

Teachings

  • Serve humanity as God
  • Focus on strength and positivity
  • Education is the key to success
